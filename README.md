# Soundborn

**Genre:** Turn-Based RPG Dungeon Crawler  
**Platform:** iOS  
**Engine:** Unity (2D)  
**Created by:** [masonliiu](https://github.com/masonliiu)

**Soundborn** takes you to a universe built from music, where living manifestations of musical genres must defend their world from the encroaching force of Silence. Tap, strategize, and summon genre heroes to climb the corrupted tower and defeat the Silence.

**Key Features:**
- Collect musical characters, each with unique mechanics
- Team building with genre strategy and synergy
- Turn-based combat and creative team combinations

---

## Core Gameplay

1. **Summon/Unlock** new musical characters
2. **Assemble a Team** (3–5 Soundborn)
3. **Enter Tower** and select a floor
4. **Encounter Enemies & Bosses**
5. **Engage in Turn-Based Combat**
6. **Earn Rewards:** keys, fragments, album shards
7. **Upgrade Characters** & unlock new abilities
8. **Progress Higher** up the tower

---

## Character System

Every character in Soundborn features a:

- **Genre:** Defines identity and abilities (e.g. Jazz, Death Metal)
- **Class/Role:** DPS, Support, Tank, etc.
- **Element Type:** Unique musical elements (Bass, Percussion, Harmony, Noise, Melody, Synth)

**Examples:**

| Character      | Role        | Element   | Description                       |
| -------------- | ----------- | --------- | --------------------------------- |
| Jazz           | Support DPS | Harmony   | Combo-extending buffs             |
| Rock           | DPS         | Bass      | High single-hit damage            |
| Hip-Hop        | Support     | Percussion| Turn-order manipulation           |
| EDM            | Burst DPS   | Synth     | Delayed explosive attacks         |
| Classical      | Support     | Harmony   | Buff & debuff utility             |
| Lo-Fi          | Healer      | Harmony   | Sustained healing aura            |

---

## Elements System

**EXAMPLES**
- **BASS:** Power, heaviness, sustain  
  *Genres: Metal, Hip-Hop, Dubstep*
- **PERCUSSION:** Rhythm, beat, speed  
  *Genres: Techno, Drum & Bass, Breakcore*
- **HARMONY:** Progressions, support  
  *Genres: Jazz, Soul, Classical, Bedroom Pop*
- **NOISE:** Chaos, distortion  
  *Genres: Shoegaze, Industrial, Black Metal*
- **MELODY:** Tunes, hooks, burst  
  *Genres: K-pop, Pop, J-Rock, EDM Melodic*
- **SYNTH:** Electronic, modulation  
  *Genres: Synthwave, Vaporwave, Trance*

**Elemental Affinities (Combat Triangle):**

- Bass > Synth
- Synth > Harmony
- Harmony > Noise
- Noise > Melody
- Melody > Percussion
- Percussion > Bass

---

## Combat System

- **Turn Order:** Determined by Speed stat
- **Actions per Turn:** Basic attack, Special ability (when charged), Ultimate (when charged)
- **Sample Abilities:**  
  - *Rock: Power Strike, Ear-piercer, Amplifier (Ultimate)*
  - *Jazz: Blue Note, Swing Buff, Solo Run (Ultimate)*
  - *Hip-Hop: Spit Verse, Off-Beat, Take the Stage (Ultimate)*

- Level up characters
- Unlock and upgrade skills
- Equip instruments, accessories, tuning mods

**Equipment Example:**  
Golden Sax (Jazz), Steel-String Guitar (Rock), Analog Synth Core (EDM), Metronome of Discipline (Classical)

## Summoning System

Collect:
- Sound Keys
- Album Shards
- Genre Tokens

**Summoning Animation:**  
A record player or signal tuner spins, birthing new Soundborn from the waveform.
